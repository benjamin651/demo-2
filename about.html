Our Story

Founded in 2010 by landscape professional Michael Johnson, BluePeak began as a small operation with one truck and a commitment to exceptional service.

What started as a neighborhood lawn mowing service has grown into a full-scale property care company serving residential and commercial clients throughout the region.

Our growth has been organic, built on word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied clients who appreciate our reliability and attention to detail.
